## Assumptions and Limitations

### Assumptions
- Interest rate remains constant throughout the loan period
- Penalty interest is calculated using simple interest
- EMI repayment structure is followed
- Missed instalments are not compounded

### Limitations
- Member-wise tracking is not implemented (group-level focus)
- Does not handle changing interest rates
- Not a substitute for a Core Banking System (CBS)

This tool is intended for calculation support and decision-making assistance at branch level.
